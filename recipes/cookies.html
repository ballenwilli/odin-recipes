Ingredients

Recipe for no-bake cookies

  • 2 cups of Sugar
  • 2 tbsps of cocoa
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 2 cups of quick oats
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 tsp of vanilla

Directions:

  1. Add sugar, cocoa, milk, and butter to pan and mix well
  2. Bring mixture to a rolling boil(Medium Heat)
  3. After 150 seconds remove from heat
  4. Quickly add other ingredients and stir well
  5. Dip out cookies with a spoon onto a wax sheet
  6. Let cool, then overeat
